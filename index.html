Welcome to Little Lemon

Nestled in the heart of Chicago, Illinois, Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant that blends tradition with innovation. Inspired by Italian, Greek, and Turkish cuisine, our chefs curate a dynamic menu of seasonal delights. From savory souvlaki to aromatic risottos, each dish reflects the diverse flavors of the Mediterranean region.

Meet Mario and Adrian, the visionary brothers behind our culinary journey. Mario, our masterful chef, brings generations of family recipes and Italian culinary expertise to every creation. Adrian, our marketing maven, led the expansion of our menu to encompass the rich tapestry of Mediterranean flavors.

Step into our rustic yet inviting ambiance, where every visit feels like coming home. Join us at Little Lemon and experience the magic of Mediterranean dining reimagined.

Greek Salad with Grilled Chicken

A classic Greek salad featuring crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, cucumber slices, red onions, Kalamata olives, and feta cheese, tossed in a light vinaigrette dressing. Serve it with tender grilled chicken breast slices seasoned with Mediterranean herbs for a protein-packed and refreshing meal.

Mediterranean Veggie Wrap

A flavorful wrap filled with roasted vegetables such as eggplant, zucchini, bell peppers, and cherry tomatoes, seasoned with garlic and olive oil. Add a spread of creamy hummus and a sprinkle of crumbled feta cheese, then wrap it all up in a warm tortilla. This vegetarian option is both satisfying and healthy.

Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta

A simple yet elegant pasta dish featuring succulent shrimp sautéed in a lemon garlic butter sauce. Toss the shrimp with al dente pasta noodles, fresh parsley, and a squeeze of lemon juice. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and cracked black pepper for a burst of flavor. This dish is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.
